Loan Lifecycle

Step 1 - Create Loan

Borrower submits a loan request. The loan status becomes PENDING.

Step 2 - Approval

An authorized LAZ address approves or rejects the request.

  • APPROVED
  • REJECTED

Step 3 - Disbursement

Omnibus confirms the loan disbursement. The loan status becomes DISBURSED.

Step 4 - Repayment

Borrower repays the loan through omnibus processing. Outstanding balance decreases until fully repaid.
