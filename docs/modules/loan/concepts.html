Loan Module Concepts

The Loan module manages the lifecycle of loans issued to borrowers under a controlled policy framework.

Loan

A loan represents a borrowing agreement recorded on-chain.

Field Description
id Unique identifier of the loan
borrower Address requesting the loan
laz Authority responsible for approving the loan
omnibus Authority responsible for confirming disbursement and repayment
principal Initial loan amount
outstanding Remaining amount to be repaid
tenor_months Duration of the loan

Actors

Borrower

The borrower submits loan requests and receives the loan funds.

LAZ Authority

Authorized policy actor responsible for approving or rejecting loans.

Omnibus Authority

Responsible for confirming disbursement and recording repayments.

Governance

Controls module parameters such as loan limits and authorized actors.

Loan Status

Status Description
PENDING Loan request submitted
APPROVED Loan approved by LAZ
DISBURSED Funds disbursed to borrower
REPAID Loan fully repaid
REJECTED Loan rejected
CANCELLED Disbursement rejected
